Luqua - Support

Real-time AI form feedback for your workouts

Luqua is a fitness application that uses your device's camera and on-device machine learning to analyze your body posture in real time, provide exercise form feedback, count repetitions, and track your workout history — all processed and stored locally on your device.

2. Frequently Asked Questions

Does Luqua record or upload my camera?
No. All camera frames are processed entirely on your device in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Do I need an account?
No. Luqua works without any sign-up, login, or internet connection.

Why does the app need camera permission?
The camera is used solely to detect your body posture so Luqua can give real-time form feedback and count your reps. You can revoke it at any time in your device settings.

How do I delete my data?
Open Luqua and go to Settings → Data → Delete user data, or simply uninstall the app.
