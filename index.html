Frequently Asked Questions

Does Luqua record or upload my camera?
No. All camera frames are processed entirely on your device in real time. Nothing is recorded, stored, or sent to any server.

Do I need an account?
No. Luqua works without any sign-up or account.

Which exercises are supported?
Push-up, Squat, Plank, Pull-up, Lunge, Glute Bridge, Calf Raise, and more.

Why does the app need camera permission?
The camera is used only to detect your body posture so Luqua can give real-time form feedback and count your reps.
